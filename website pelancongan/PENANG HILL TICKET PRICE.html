Operation Hours

Effective from 26th March 2021, the funicular train and ticketing counter operating hour as per below:
Mon - Thurs Funicular Service 6.30am to 8.00pm
(Last trip from Upper Station to Lower Station)
Ticketing Counter 6.15am to 6.45pm
Fri - Sun Funicular Service 6.30am to 9.00pm
(Last trip from Upper Station to Lower Station)
Ticketing Counter 6.15am to 7.45pm
  • The management reserves the rights to revise the schedule of the operating hours and the tickets pricing without prior notice
  • PHC at this moment offers only Sunrise rate of RM3.00/round trip to Malaysians (MyKad and MyKid holders) at certain days and times. To enjoy this special rate, you are required to purchase tickets on-site from 6.30am until 8.00am.
  • Sunrise & Sunset rates or any other promotional tickets are for Walk-In ONLY. Kindly proceed to the ticketing counters at Lower Station to purchase the ticket. These special rates cannot be combined with other promotion. PHC reserves the right to change the terms and conditions without prior notice.
  • Sunrise & Sunset rates are only applicable to Malaysia citizen (MyKad and MyKid holders only). (Click here to view the calendar of Sunrise & Sunset rates)
  • Sunrise & Sunset rates, train chartering and compartment booking are not available during long weekends, festive seasons, school holidays and peak seasons.
